1. Robinhood Login Issues – Step-by-Step Guidance

Common Reasons

  • Incorrect email address or password
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) or OTP issues
  • Temporary account lock for security reasons
  • Browser or mobile app errors

How to Fix Login Issues

  • Update the Robinhood mobile app or refresh the website
  • Make sure you are using the registered email address
  • Use the "Forgot Password" option if needed
  • Disable VPN or proxy connections
  • Check email inbox and spam folder for alerts

2. Withdrawal Issues (Funds Not Received)

Possible Causes

  • Bank account verification pending
  • Withdrawal holding or settlement period
  • Compliance or security review
  • Incorrect or mismatched bank details

Step-by-Step Solution

  • Ensure your linked bank account is fully verified
  • Check withdrawal status in transaction history
  • Wait 3–5 business days for processing
  • Confirm bank details are accurate

3. Deposit Issues (Amount Not Credited)

Deposit delays usually occur due to banking processing times or account-related restrictions.

  • Save your transaction reference or confirmation number
  • Confirm the transaction with your bank
  • Review deposit processing timelines
  • Check for any account limitations or restrictions

4. Account Restricted or Locked

  • Incomplete or pending identity verification (KYC)
  • Unusual login or trading activity detected
  • Policy or compliance-related reviews

Account reviews may take several business days. Please follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

🔍 What is an Online Scam?

Online scams are fraudulent activities where scammers try to trick users into giving money, personal information, or access to their devices. These scams often use fear, urgency, or fake offers.

Example: “Your subscription has been charged. Call now to cancel.”

Why Awareness Matters?

Millions of people lose money every year due to scams. Awareness is the best protection against fraud.

⚠️ Common Types of Scams

1. Fake Email Scams

  • Fake invoices
  • Subscription renewal alerts
  • Refund offers

2. Fake Ads

  • “Call now for refund” ads
  • Urgent warning messages

3. Fake Websites

  • Look like real websites
  • Ask for login or payment details

4. Tech Support Scams

  • Ask for remote access
  • Claim your device is infected

🎯 Keywords Scammers Target

  • refund support
  • cancel subscription
  • customer service number
  • billing issue help

🚨 Red Flags

  • Urgent messages
  • Unknown numbers
  • Spelling mistakes
  • Suspicious links
  • Requests for payment or access

✅ How to Stay Safe

Step 1: Stay Calm

Do not panic when you see urgent messages.

Step 2: Verify Information

Always check official websites directly.

Step 3: Do Not Share Details

Never share OTP, passwords, or bank details.

Step 4: Avoid Unknown Calls

Do not call numbers from suspicious messages.

Step 5: Report Scams

Report scams to your local cyber crime authority.

🔒 Extra Safety Tips

  • Use antivirus software
  • Enable spam filters
  • Avoid clicking unknown links
  • Keep your system updated

🛠️ Safe Tech Support Setup Guide

Step 1: Use Official Website

Always visit the official website directly instead of clicking on ads or unknown links.

Step 2: Verify Subscription

Check your account dashboard to confirm any subscription or billing details.

Step 3: Use Secure Payment Methods

Only use trusted payment options and avoid sharing card details over phone calls.

Step 4: Avoid Third-Party Numbers

Do not trust random support numbers found in ads or emails.

Step 5: Keep Records

Maintain receipts and confirmation emails for your purchases.

⚠️ Common Scam Methods

1. Fake Emails

  • Subscription renewal alerts
  • Fake invoices
  • Refund messages

2. Fake Ads

  • “Call now” ads
  • Refund offers

3. Fake Websites

  • Look like real sites
  • Ask for personal info

4. Call Scams

  • Ask for remote access
  • Request money transfer

🚨 Red Flags

  • Urgent warnings
  • Unknown phone numbers
  • Spelling mistakes
  • Suspicious links
  • Requests for payment

✅ Safety Tips

  • Never share OTP or passwords
  • Do not allow remote access
  • Check official sources
  • Use antivirus software
  • Avoid clicking unknown links

What To Do If You Suspect a Scam?

  • Do not respond
  • Do not call unknown numbers
  • Report to cyber crime authorities
  • Check bank statements
